About Yellow Captain

Yellow Captain, the Pizza Specialist, is a tastefully decorated, softly lit place at Vision City Mall offering pizza, pasta, salad and dessert. Our philosophy is simple — less is more, so toppings shine without too many competing flavors or too much cheese.

Enjoy friendly atmosphere, good service, and reasonable prices. You’re likely to dine with a who’s who of PNG’s movers and shakers.
